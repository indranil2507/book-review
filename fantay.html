The Harry Potter Series


The wonderful story of a wizarding school named hogwarts and its students made a huge place in the heart of its readers very soon. Written by J.k Rowling thi series revolves around the magical adventures of a boy name harry and his friends. The reception from the audience was so huge that the author succeded in becoming a Billionaire by seeling the harry potter books. the author had written in her book that harry was so special that everyone in the wizarding world shal know his name, however what she didnt expect was that every one even in the real world shall know of Harry Potter.






Twilight series

Twilight by Stephaine Meyer is sometimes referred to as a shakespearean love story with monster and myths. A love story between Edward and Bella,a vampire and a human has gained a huge response from young-adult readers. The series of 4 books with the legends of vampires and werewolves did not take much time to be one of reader's favourite.






The Chronicles Of Narnia

A story of four teenagers who become kings and queens in the magical land of Narnia was written by C.S.Lewis. The youngest of the family Lucy discovers a wardrobe and finally takes her siblings Edmund,Peter and Susan to Narnia where discover strange things like talking animal and othe strange creatures. The story revolves around how the four siblings become the rulers of Narnia and save the magical kingdom whenever called to do so.






The lord of the rings

One Ring to rule them all, One Ring to find them, One Ring to bring them all and in the darkness bind them. A story set in the prehistoric era when mythological characters like elves, hobbits and dwarves roamed the earth. Stories of wars between good and evil with the aim of obtaining the rings, written by J.R.R Tolkien this series of three books was inspired from his own life when he fought in 2 world wars in which he learned that sacrifice is necessary for a greater good.
