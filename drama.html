Tuesdays with Moorie


Tuesdays with Morrie is a memoir by American author Mitch Albom about a series of visits Albom made to his former sociology professor Morrie Schwartz, as Schwartz gradually dies of ALS. The book teaches us various life lessons through these student teacher conversations and is a joy to read.






The Alchemist

The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho is a book about a shepherd who takes on a journey to fulfill a dream, the entire story is full of optimistic bursts which makes it a must read. It teaches us that the journey to reach ones's destiny is as important as the destiny itself.






The Book Thief

The Book Thief by Mark Zusak is not the kind of story that has you crying one moment and laughing the next. The sadness surrounding Liesel's story creeps up on you, until suddenly you realise that it was like this for so many, and how serious it was, and is. In reflection, The Book Thief leaves behind a sense of guilt, in some ways






The Kite Runner

A gripping and emotional story of betrayal and redemption is one of the first of Khalid Hosseini, a story of two young boys Amir and Hassan and how they try to win the kite flying competition that year. However war breaks out and the country becomeas a very dangerous place. Amir and his father are forced to flee and the kite runner becomes the story of Amir's redemption for his actions as a kid in Kabul.
