The Fault in Our Stars


A story of two cancer patients fighting against the odds and trying to find love and comfort in each other, the story penned down by John Green takes Agustus and Hazel through the streets of Amsterdam where they find solace in themselves. A book that makes you giggle, smile and finally cry as you realise how valuable love is and how just open person can change your life in the best possible way.






Till the Last Breathe

Probably one of Durjoy Datta’s best works so far, the book tells you a story of two docors and two critically ill patients who are being treated by them and ultimately how each of them find a very different form of love. A hearwarming story from the PoVs of the patients, Pihu and Dushyant and the doctors Armaan and Zarah.






I too Had a Love Story

The story of Khusi and Ravin narrated through the words of Ravinder Singh. Two strangers who find each other and eventually fall in love through a matrimonial site. The sweet love story however ends in a twisted turn of fate and changes the life of our protagonists in an unimaginable way.






A walk to Remember

A walk to remember by Nicholas Sparks is truly a masterpiece in the genre of light romance. Its a sad story which is actually happy with its journey. A story about two opposites and how the fall in love with each other as you fall in love with their journey. .
